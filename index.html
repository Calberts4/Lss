1969

1968 the band Smile was formed by Brian May. It included Tim Staffell as lead singer and bass guitarist, and later, drummer Roger Taylor. The in 1969 Staffell left the band leading to Farrokh (Freddie) Bulsara to become the new lead vocalist

1971

After a name change from Smile the band Queen was born with guitarist Brian May, drummer Roger Taylor, vocalist and some time piano player Freddie Mercury, and bassist John Deacon.

Jul 13st, 1973

Queen's debut album "Queen" came out in '73 with a combo of glam and hard rock songs.

Nov 8st, 1974

After releasing their third album, Queen hit it big hitting number two in the UK and just missing the USA top ten.

Nov 21st, 1975

With the release of “Night at the Opera” the album shoots to number five in the USA and number one in the UK and stayed there for nine weeks. With its single “Bohemian Rhapsody.”

Dec 10th, 1976

A Day At The Races is released and with another hit "Somebody To Love".

Oct 28th, 1977

With the release of “We Will Rock You” and “We Are The Champions” the album News of the World it the top ten again.

Jan 1st, 1980

In the 1980's Queen was on top of the world winning the Dick Clark Award for best band. As well as winning a Juno award for best band.

Feb 27th, 1984

Queen single "I want to break free" went gold in the US and silver in the UK.

Jul 13th, 1985

Queen performed a short, 20-minute set at Live Aid. and Queen were unanimously voted - by press and public alike - as the band that stole the show. And even their set was considered the best rock performance ever.

May 30th, 1991

The game album is relesed and hits gold in the UK and 4x Platinum in the US.

Nov 23th, 1991

Freddie Mercury confirms rumors that he does have AIDS.

Nov 24th, 1991

The next day after comfirming that he has AIDS Freddie Mercury dies at the age of 45.

May 20th 1992

The remaining members of Queen along with other famous performers hold a tribute consert in the name of Freddie Mercury to raise money for AIDS research.

Nov 6th, 1995

Last recorded album with Freddie Murcury is relesed 4 years after his death.
